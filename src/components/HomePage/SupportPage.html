Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to QuizCore Support.Our goal is to provide a smooth and user-friendly platform to our users in order to optimize the user experience. Our team is available 24/7 for your assistance and you can contact our team via different platforms mentioned under Contact US.These are the most frequently asked questions by our users.

How to register for exams?

To register for exams, visit the "Exams" section under your account dashboard. Choose the desired exam, select the date, and confirm your registration. You will receive an email confirmation with all the details.


How long will it take to release results?

The exam results are typically released within 7-10 business days after the exam date. You will receive a notification via email once the results are available. You can also check your results in your account dashboard under the "Results" section.

How to get technical assistance?

If you are facing technical difficulties, you can contact our support team via email. You can also visit the "Contact Us" page and browse through the options available for further assistance.


How to select correct exam?

To select the correct exam,log in to your account, visit the "Exams" section, and filter the available exams by subject or date. Read the descriptions carefully to ensure you choose the correct exam based on your course or qualification.
